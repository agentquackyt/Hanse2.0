
# Hanse 2.0

## Introduction

Northern Europe, 1241 AD. The Baltic Sea is alive with the creak of timber ships and the distant clang of harbor bells. Merchant cities, bound together by the secretive Hanseatic League, thrive on trade, alliances, and ambition. From Lübeck, proud ships set sail, their holds brimming with grain, timber, cloth, and fur—goods destined for distant shores and eager markets.

But the League is no open guild. Its power is guarded by a web of favors, debts, and silent agreements. Outsiders are kept at bay, and only the shrewdest find a way in.

Yet, in the shadow of these great merchants, a new story begins. You are a young trader, owner of a single ship, a handful of gold, and dreams as vast as the Baltic itself. The League may be closed, but the sea is open - and fortune favors the bold.

> Hanse 2.0 is an economic trading simulation inspired by the legendary Hanseatic League. Step into the world introduced above, where every voyage is a risk, every deal a story, and every port a new opportunity. Will you rise from obscurity to become a legend of the League?

## Gameplay

- **Trading**: Buy goods where they are cheap and sell them where demand is high. The dynamic market system adjusts prices based on supply, demand, and local production.
- **Navigation**: Command your ship to travel across the map between historical cities to uncover profitable trade routes. Click your ship's destination carefully, as you can only travel along connected nodes.
- **Dynamic Economy & Shadow Producers**: Prices evolve dynamically as the economy reacts to your trades, city satisfaction levels, and autonomous "shadow producers" that consume and supply goods in the background.
- **Kontors**: Establish trading posts (Kontors) in key cities. Having a Kontor allows you to view market status and conduct trade in that city even without a docked ship. You start of with a Kontor
in Lübeck, this is also where your ship is at the beginning of the game.

## Controls

The game is controlled with your mouse and keyboard modifiers:

- **Left Click on a City**: Open the city's market & overview modal. (Available if your ship is docked there or if you have a Kontor in the city).
- **Shift + Left Click on a City**: Travel – navigate your ship directly to the selected harbor.
- **Right Mouse Button + Drag**: Pan the map canvas.
- **Scroll Wheel**: Zoom the map canvas in and out.
- **Market Interface**: Drag the buy/sell slider in the city modal to trade goods between your ship's inventory (or Kontor) and the active city's market.
